Return

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, or the unit is found to be defective, you may return the item to us in its within 15 days.
Only items purchased directly from the online store (or crowdfunding channels) will be accepted. For products purchased from retailers, please get in touch with them directly for returns.
Returned items must be in original packaging, including any accessories, manuals.
Non-returnable items include items purchased through retailers, seed packs, and damaged products through customer mishandling.
Returns made without a receipt may be refused. Refunds are completed usually within 12 to 15 business days. All refunds will be issued based on your original form of payment method.
For returned items, a full refund will be credited for defective products. For non-defective items, refund excludes all shipping and handling fees.

We are here to help anytime. Contact our Customer Support team.
